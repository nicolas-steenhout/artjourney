Busied my hands by sketching my father's Scotch dresser, now mine.

![Line and wash sketch. A wooden chest of drawer. There are three big drawers below a set of 2 square drawers on each side and two small drawers one above another.](/images/26-03-18-scotch-dresser.png)

He calls it an <span lang="fr">Armoire Normande</span>. Wife refers to it as a Scotch chest or dresser. I don't care what it's called. It's beautiful and I love it.
