Medical Aid in Dying is a heavy topic. But it's a topic I'm passionate about.

![Line and wash sketch. Two people in a drab office. One is wearing a suit and sitting behind a desk. The other is in a wheelchair facing the desk. I've long opposed the legalization of physician assisted suicide, euthanasia, or MAiD (Medical Aid in Dying). I'm not talking about it as often as I used to, but you can find the post about it in the [February 15, 2026 edition of the newsletter](https://buttondown.com/nic-steenhout/archive/maid-ableism-cognitive-accessibility-and-more/)
